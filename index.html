Whether you are looking for a wildlife or cultural experience, traveling on a family holiday, with friends, or solo, we hope you all join us as we explore the shores of Lake Victoria or Lake Bunyonyi, trek the Rwenzori, Kilimanjaro or Elgon mountain ranges, or search for lions out on the golden plains of one or more of our pristine national parks. Be it budget, mid-range, or luxury, Nature Adventure Africa Safaris is the number one provider of tailored-made tours and safaris in East and Central Africa. Most importantly, we operate out of concern for the future of African wildlife conservation. As such, we encourage low-impact tourism and small scale alternatives to mass tourism.

As dedicated conservationists, we want your African safari to be as informative and educational as it is adventurous.We want you to understand the challenges, successes, and hopes for the people and wildlife of this beautiful place we call home. Book with us and we shall show you the best of mountain gorillas in Uganda and Maasai Mara National Reserve in Kenya and offer 07 Day Uganda Kenya Safari and our price quote include one way domestic Aero Link flight from Entebbe to Maasai Mara National Reserve in Kenya. We invite you to take a look at some of our packages, and hope you’ll get in touch to plan the African safari of a lifetime!
